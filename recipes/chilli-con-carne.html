Chili Con Carne

Delicious Chilli con Carne

Servings: 6

Time: 1h 15m

Ingredients

  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 Celery Sticks
  • 1 Bell Peppers
  • 1 Can Chickpeas (400g)
  • 1 Can Kidney Beans (400g)
  • 2 Can Corn (400g)
  • 2 Cans Chopped Tomatoes/ Tomato sauce (400g)
  • 500g Minced Meat
  • 500g Yogurt
  • 2 cups Rice
  • Coriander
  • Chili
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Cinnamon
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Peel and dice the onions, celery, carrots and the bell peppers
  2. Start frying the chili and spices (cumin, cayenne and cinnamon) in a pot
  3. Add the diced ingredients and the garlic and let it fry for around 7min (don't forget to salt it too)
  4. Add the minced meat and begin frying it
  5. Add the kidney beans, chickpeas, corn and tomato sauce
  6. Bring the chili to a boil, then let it simmer (the best is if it simmers for an hour, but if you're hungry, just leave it for a bit)
  7. Cook the rice while the chili simmers
  8. Garnish the end with the coriander and add a bowl of yogurt on the side which you can put on the chili.
