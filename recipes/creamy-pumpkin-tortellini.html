Creamy Pumpkin Tortellini

Delicious Creamy Pumpkin Tortellini

Servings: 6

Time: 30m

Ingredients

  • 500g Tortellini
  • 1 tbs Butter
  • 3 tbs Chopped Onions
  • 1 cup Mashed Pumpkin
  • 1 cup Cream
  • 1 tbs Minced Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the pumpkin in a little bit of water until soft, then mash when ready
  2. Start to cook the pasta
  3. Meanwhile, in a large pan, heat butter over medium heat. Add the onion
  4. Add pumpkin and nutmeg; cook and stir 1 minute longer. Stir in cream; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer, uncovered, 4-5 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and spice
  5. Add the pasta; toss with sauce, adding enough reserved pasta water to coat pasta
